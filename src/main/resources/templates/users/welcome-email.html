Welcome to the Canopy! We are thrilled to have you join our community of researchers, analysts, and scientists dedicated to advancing biomedical research.

Canopy is a secure, cloud-based platform for accessing and analyzing curated, de-identified research datasets. It features a public-facing study search, enhanced study overview pages with metadata and variable information, and integrated tools to support your research workflow. Whether you are exploring datasets, conducting in-depth analyses, or collaborating with other researchers, Canopy provides the resources to meet your needs.

To help you get started, we recommend checking out the following resources:

  • Canopy Tutorial
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Resource Center

These links will guide you through navigating Canopy and accessing the datasets you need for your research.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please do not hesitate to reach out to our support team.

We look forward to seeing the incredible insights and discoveries you will make.
